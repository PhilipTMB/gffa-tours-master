Our Project

Hello, and welcome to Recyclopedia-- Sparbots White's research project. We hope that, with the help of this new website, we can teach you all that we know about recycling. This project began with the disappointment we felt when observing the recycling cans in our cafeteria-- at least 50% of all plastic bottles were thrown away and the rest were still covered in food materials when actually recycled. So, we took "Before" and "After" photos. The first pictures were before we put up prompts over the cans. The second photos were the recycled amount after a day with signs up.
